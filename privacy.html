Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data elun.me ("we", "us") collects when you use the Service, and how it's used.

1. What we collect

DataWhy
Birth date, time, location, genderRequired to calculate your BaZi chart — this is the core input to the Service.
Name (optional)Personalizes the report if you choose to provide it.
Email addressTo match your purchase to your report claim, and for support.
Order numberTo verify your purchase with our payment provider, Paddle.

We do not require an account or collect passwords. We don't run advertising trackers on elun.me.

2. How report generation works

Your birth data is used to compute your chart deterministically (no third party involved in that step), then passed to a language model (Anthropic's Claude API) to write the narrative portions of your report. Anthropic processes this data as our sub-processor to generate the report text and does not use it to train models. The finished report is stored so you can reopen it later via your permanent link.

3. Payment data

We never see or store your card details. Checkout and billing are handled entirely by Paddle.com Market Limited, acting as Merchant of Record — see Paddle's Privacy Policy for how they handle payment and billing data.

4. Data retention

We keep your chart inputs and generated report tied to your order so you can re-open or re-generate it later. If you'd like your data deleted, email us (see below) and we'll remove it, subject to any records we're required to keep for tax/accounting purposes.

5. Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time by emailing hello@elun.me. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable law (e.g. GDPR, CCPA).

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@elun.me.
