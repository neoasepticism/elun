A Strong Earth self that discharges through refined Metal reads as the constitutional-law path: organizing, drafting, building through system.

Trump's chart carries too much Fire (Resource) piled on already-heavy Earth. His signature: “when Metal and Water arrive — the elements structurally absent — the chart converts its enormous surplus into worldly result.” The Mystic structure runs on image and intuition, not institution; the same surplus, lacking the internal Metal-Water to channel it, waits for the years that supply what the chart lacks. When Metal-Water arrive by luck pillar, the surplus finally converts.
