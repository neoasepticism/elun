IThe Chart

The Day Master is 丙 Bing Fire — the sun itself in Chinese metaphysical tradition: radiating, centralizing, impossible to ignore. He arrives in a Wu-Yin (戊寅) month, the first breath of spring when Wood is rising and Fire has not yet found its full strength. The chart is near-balanced but tips mildly weak: an ally-score of 3.0 against a drain-score of 3.5, with Earth (Output) commanding fully half the chart's mass, Wood (Resource) holding a third, and Fire (Peers) only seventeen percent. Metal and Water — Wealth and Officer — are absent from the natal chart entirely. The structure is Mystic, keyed to the month branch's Yin Wood hidden in 寅, pointing to a creative intelligence that absorbs and transforms rather than simply executes. The Useful Element is Wood as primary Resource, with Fire as secondary support; Water and Metal remain unfavorable throughout a life, because they suppress an already under-fueled flame. The single mechanism the audit will test is this: every decade that brings Wood or Fire is, by the chart's internal logic, a decade when the Day Master finds traction — creative output, recognition, and the capacity to generate. Every decade that delivers Water or Metal presses against the grain. The chart carries almost no Wealth or Officer energy natively, which classically describes someone for whom institutional hierarchy and conventional accumulation are foreign languages learned late and imperfectly. The dominant ten-god is Creator (Output/Expression), appearing twice, with Maverick, Mystic, and Scholar each present once. There is no Entrepreneur, no Governor, no Steward in the natal pillars. This is a chart built to make things and impose a vision — not to manage, comply, or consolidate.

IIThe Day Master

Bing Fire on a Chen (辰) day branch carries a particular weight: the Dragon earth contains hidden within it a Scholar stem (乙 Wood), a Governor stem (癸 Water), and a Creator stem (戊 Earth). The day pillar is therefore quietly complex — a sun resting on soil that holds both the nourishment it needs (Scholar/Wood) and a dampening agent (Governor/Water) that can, when luck pillars activate it, turn supportive ground into something corrosive. The birth season of early spring aligns with the Resource role of Wood, which means the chart is structurally disposed toward learning, inspiration, and the conversion of received ideas into new forms — calligraphy absorbed and later transmuted into typeface design is almost too clean an example. The distribution of Archetypes is telling in its absences as much as its presences: two Creators, one Maverick, one Mystic, one Scholar. No Challenger (power-seeker), no Entrepreneur (asset-builder), no Steward (consolidator). The ten-god landscape is almost entirely internal — expression, resource absorption, nonconformity. The person this chart describes operates through the force of a synthesizing intelligence and an output drive that is constitutionally stronger than any appetite for political maneuvering or institutional patience. When the biography shows him repeatedly colliding with organizations and hierarchies, the chart offers a structural explanation: the Archetypes for working within power simply are not present in the natal configuration.

IVThe Signature

The repeating pattern across all seven documented decades is a counterpoint between Output and Resource — between the chart's enormous expressive drive (Earth at fifty percent, two Creator archetypes) and its dependence on Wood to keep the fire alive. The decades when Wood arrived at the stem (乙亥, 甲戌) were the decades of transformation and return. The decades when Water arrived at the stem (丙子's hidden Water harmony, 癸酉, 壬申) were the decades of maximum external pressure, and within those pressures the output intensified rather than diminished — which is itself a Bing Fire quality: the sun burns brightest when something tries to obscure it. The chart never resolved this tension. It simply lived inside it. If the chart has one sentence about this life, it is: a mildly weak sun in early spring, with no Wealth and no Officer in its natal bones, sustained itself entirely by converting every available resource into light — and the decades that tried to extinguish it produced, paradoxically, the most enduring illumination.

VThe Final Decade

Steve Jobs died in October 2011, at age 56, placing his death in the final weeks of the 47~56 luck pillar — on the threshold of the 壬申 decade that never truly became his to inhabit. The 辛未 pillar (67~76), which the engine marks as current in the computational sense, is a decade he did not live to enter. Rather than project forward, the audit notes what the final documented decade demonstrated: that a chart's most structurally adverse configuration — two consecutive pillars of Governor and Challenger Water, Metal activating the Water channel, the month branch under clash — did not arrest the creative and institutional output. The last years produced the iPhone, the iPad, and the world's most valuable company. The chart, at its end, was under maximum pressure and generating maximum light. That is the final entry in this audit.

Method & limits. What this audit demonstrates is that a BaZi chart, read honestly against public biography, can illuminate the structural rhythm of a life — the quality of decades, the nature of reversals, the relationship between a person's elemental constitution and the historical pressures they encountered — without claiming to have predicted specific events. What it does not demonstrate is causation, inevitability, or the reduction of an singular human life to elemental mechanics; the biographical record repeatedly exceeded what the chart mechanics alone would forecast. The birth hour, if ever documented, would add a fourth pillar and potentially clarify the exact weight of the Officer and Wealth elements — and might refine the strength calculation — but it would not rewrite the central audit finding: this was a Resource-hungry Yang Fire chart that spent its life converting Wood into light, and the record confirms it did exactly that. Pillars, strength, structure and every clash/combination named above were computed deterministically by Elun's engine from the public birth date; the interpretation layer matched them against public biography. This is cultural analysis of a public figure for reflection and entertainment — not advice, and not a claim about anyone's future.
