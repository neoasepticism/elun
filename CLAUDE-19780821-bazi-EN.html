· Why this chart is correct 精密

Most BaZi tools — and general AI chatbots — read the clock time as-is. But Korea's clock runs on the 135°E meridian (Japan), while Seoul sits at 127°E — roughly 32 minutes apart. A birth logged at 05:00 is, by the true position of the sun, 04:25. Since 05:00 falls on the boundary between the Yin (寅, 03–05) and Mao (卯, 05–07) hours, this correction determines the Hour Pillar — one full quarter of the chart. This report corrects for true solar time and longitude and computes every pillar deterministically from the Julian day. The same input always yields the same chart.
