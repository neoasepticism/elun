The Essence of 戊戌

The fortress, twice earth: the most immovable seat in the sixty. 戊戌 guards — people, principles, gates — and its loyalty survives everything except betrayal from inside the walls.

The stem: Wu is the mountain: massive, dependable, unmoved by weather. It absorbs pressure that cracks others and becomes the load-bearing wall of any group it joins.

Appearance & Presence

The fortress: square, guarded features, a gatekeeper's steadiness — trust made visible.

The Wu base — solid broad frame, strong nose, calm weighty presence and unhurried movement — shaded by the Dog: a solid, dependable look that people instinctively trust. Traditional physiognomy; enjoy as folklore, not science.

Temperament note: guardian loyalty — you keep the gate, keep the faith, and keep receipts.

In Love

In love you need an equal, not an admirer or a caretaker. Side-by-side partnership — two whole people walking the same road — suits you far better than fusion.

Work & Direction

You work best with ownership: your own desk, your own targets, your own name on the result. Independence is not a perk for you; it is a requirement.
