丁亥

The Ding Hai Day

Ding · Yin Fire sitting on the Pig · deep water · early winter

“The lamp over night water — intuitive and romantic; sees in the dark.”

◆ LUCKY · Nobleman 天乙貴人
Five-element composition — day stem 50% · hidden stems 50%

The Essence of 丁亥

A lamp over night water: intuition lit from below. 丁亥 sees in the dark — moods, motives, futures — and loves with a romantic's faith in what it sees.

The stem: Ding is aimed fire — the lantern, not the sun. It illuminates completely whatever it focuses on, perceives subtext to an uncanny degree, and warms one person at a time.

What You Sit On

正官The GovernorAuthority · order

You sit on legitimate authority — discipline is installed at the root. You respect structure, keep form, and are in turn trusted with position. Duty is not a burden you carry; it is a shape you naturally take.

Appearance & Presence

A lamp over night water: dreamy, reflective eyes; a softness that seems to see more than it says.

The Ding base — finer features with striking bright eyes — the flame shows in the gaze — shaded by the Pig: softer, fuller features and a warm, unguarded smile. Traditional physiognomy; enjoy as folklore, not science.

Temperament note: generous depth — you give from a deep well and think in oceans, not puddles.

In Love

You are a responsible, presentable, marriage-minded partner — the one parents approve of. Allow some disorder into intimacy; love is not an audit.

Work & Direction

Institutions reward you: public service, law, corporate leadership, medicine. Reputation compounds for you like interest — protect it and it will carry you.
