Appearance & Presence

Water on fire: steam-engine vitality — flushed, energetic, expressive; a face that runs warm and fast.

The Ren base — a fuller or fluid build, lively roving eyes, expansive gestures, magnetic in motion — shaded by the Horse: a bright, expressive face that hides nothing and lights easily. Traditional physiognomy; enjoy as folklore, not science.

Temperament note: visible passion — your feelings broadcast, your energy peaks fast and needs a track to run on.

In Love

You show love through reliability — remembered dates, managed logistics, a stable home. Devotion, once given, is thorough; flightiness in a partner genuinely bewilders you.

Work & Direction

Management, finance, operations, property — anywhere careful hands compound small gains into real estates. You are the person the ledger loves.
