The Essence of 己巳

A sunlit field: warm intelligence in fertile ground. 己巳 cultivates minds and moods with equal skill; its power is that everything near it ripens.

The stem: Ji is garden soil — the earth that feeds. Things and people simply grow better near it; its power works underground and compounds like richness in loam.

Appearance & Presence

A sunlit field: warm, intelligent eyes in a kind face — the look of a favorite teacher.

Temperament note: strategic heat — passion that plans, charm that calculates, patience that strikes.

In Love
