Appearance & Presence

A river through the forest: vigorous, flowing movements, a traveler's tan and readiness — motion visible at rest.

The Ren base — a fuller or fluid build, lively roving eyes, expansive gestures, magnetic in motion — shaded by the Tiger: an upright bearing with restless, forward-leaning energy. Traditional physiognomy; enjoy as folklore, not science.

Temperament note: initiative — you move first, ask later, and stagnation feels like suffocation.

In Love

You love warmly and tangibly — feeding people, making things for them, filling the room with ease. You need a partner who receives well, not one who rations joy.

Work & Direction

Craft and content are your lanes: creative work, teaching, food and hospitality, anything where the pleasure you take in making is visible in the result.
