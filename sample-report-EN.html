Water 0% — Resource. Water registers at 0% — completely absent from the natal chart. This means the quality of each decade turns almost entirely on whether the luck pillar supplies Water or Wood, and life decisions that create hydration — rest, mentorship, creative solitude, water-energy environments — carry disproportionate weight.
