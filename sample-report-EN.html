Fire 12% — Output. Fire sits at only 12%, appearing mainly as hidden stems inside the Year Branch Wu (午) — a Creator/Maverick presence that is warm but not forceful. Water 0% — Resource. Water registers at 0% — it is entirely absent from all stems, branches, and even hidden elements of this natal chart. For Yi Wood, Water is the Scholar, the primary Useful Element, the rain that feeds the vine at the root. Its total absence is the chart's most structurally significant fact: the vine grows in dry soil, and every demand from Metal (Officer) and Earth (Wealth) is being processed without the nourishment that would make carrying those demands feel natural. This gap is the lifelong background hum of the chart.
