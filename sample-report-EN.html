Earth 25% — Wealth. Earth occupies 25% of the chart as the Wealth element, appearing twice through the Steward archetype in both the year and hour stems. Water 0% — Resource. Water is entirely absent at 0%, and this is the single most defining structural fact in the chart. Without any natal Water, the Day Master carries no inherent sense of being nourished, held, or replenished; he operates on the Wood he already possesses, without the element that would continuously restore him. This absence makes Water the primary Useful Element — the most transformative force he can invite into his life.
